Welcome to

Gigapet!

Helping parents track their children's nutrition with an interactive pet

Getting our kids to eat healthy can be a daunting task or epic struggle full of chasing, throwing, and straight out refusal. With Gigapet, your child can watch their pet grow with every healthy choice they make, encouraging them to eat healthier and allowing them the chance to have fun doing it! You or your child can:

  • Choose a digital pet
  • Name their pet
  • Help the pet grow every time they eat a healthy food or take required medication
